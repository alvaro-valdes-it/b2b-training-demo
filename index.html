Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a gym membership?

Yes, for one-on-one and small group training, an active gym membership is required and is not included in the session price. Online training does not require a gym membership.

Where do the sessions take place?

In-person sessions take place at our partner gym in Miami Lakes. Online training can be done from anywhere.

Is a deposit required to book?

A deposit may be required to reserve your session time, applied toward your total package price.

What is the cancellation policy?

Please notify us at least 24 hours in advance to cancel or reschedule a session.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Zelle, cash, and card payments.

Do I need previous training experience?

No. Programs are tailored to your current fitness level, whether you're just starting out or training for years.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can move between one-on-one, small group, or online training as your schedule and goals change.
